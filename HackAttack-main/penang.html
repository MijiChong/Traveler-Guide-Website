Explore Penang

Discover the rich culture, stunning landscapes, and vibrant heritage of Malaysia's pearl.

About Penang

Penang is a Malaysian state located on the northwest coast of Peninsular Malaysia. It's known for its rich cultural diversity, beautiful beaches, and vibrant culinary scene. The state comprises Penang Island and Seberang Perai on the mainland, connected by the iconic Penang Bridge

Attractions

Escape Park

A thrilling adventure park offering zip-lining, rope courses, and water slides. Perfect for nature lovers and adrenaline junkies.

Operating Hours: Tuesday to Sunday, 10 AM - 6 PM

Ticket Prices: Vary by age group, with discounts for early bookings

Penang Hill

Offering panoramic views of the island, Penang Hill is a popular spot for hiking and nature walks.

Ticket Prices: Vary for adults, children, and seniors





Kek Lok Si Temple

One of the largest Buddhist temples in Southeast Asia, featuring a stunning pagoda and beautiful gardens.

Operating Hours: Monday - Sunday, 8.30am - 5.30pm

Entrance Fee: Free, with small charges for specific areas

St. Paul's Church

Famous for its beautiful beaches and vibrant night market.

Operating Hours: All day

Entrance Fee: Free, with small charges for specific areas




Getting There

Malacca is easily accessible by various modes of transportation. The nearest airport is Malacca International Airport, which offers domestic flights. For international travelers, the closest major airport is Kuala Lumpur International Airport, about 2 hours drive away. Malacca is also well-connected by bus and car to other major cities in Malaysia.
